Linus Torvalds

The Creator of Linux


Biography

Linus Benedict Torvalds is a Finnish software engineer who is the creator and, historically, the lead developer of the Linux kernel, used by Linux distributions and other operating systems such as Android. He also created the distributed version control system.They were later married and have three daughters, two of whom were born in the United States.
